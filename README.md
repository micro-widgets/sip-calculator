## What is SIP?

A **Systematic Investment Plan (SIP)** is a method of investing a fixed amount at regular intervals (usually monthly) in mutual funds or other securities. This helps in:
- Dollar-cost averaging (investing regardless of market conditions)
- Building discipline in investing
- Reducing the impact of market volatility
- Creating long-term wealth

## How to Use

1. **Enter Monthly Investment Amount**: The amount you want to invest every month (minimum ₹100)
2. **Enter Expected Annual Return Rate**: Your expected annual return percentage (typical range: 8%-20%)
3. **Select Time Period**: Enter the duration and select whether it's in months or years
4. **Click Calculate**: The calculator will show you:
   - Total amount you'll invest
   - Expected returns on your investment
   - Final maturity amount
   - Visual chart of your investment growth
